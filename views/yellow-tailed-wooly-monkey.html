The yellow tailed wooly monkey are one of the least known primate species.They are regularly found in the tropical andes. First discovered by scientists at the beginning of the nineteenth century, this distinct species of woolly monkey was thought to be extinct until its rediscovery in 1974. Due to polution and the destruction of their homes there are only about 250 surviving yellow tailed wooly monkeys left.
