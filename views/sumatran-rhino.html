The Sumatran Rhino

There are only about 100 Sumatran Rhinos left in the world. They are the only Asian Rhino that has two horns and are the smallest rhinos in the world. Sumatran Rhinos used live all over Asia but now they are mostly found Sumatra, Indonesia and even there habit loss due to human settlements and agriculture keeps on threatening them. In fact, the species was officially declared extinct in the wild in Malaysia on August 2015.
