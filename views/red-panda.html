In this case, Red Pandas are endangered due to deforestation (the act of destroying large amounts of trees). Their natural habitat is being demolished by people wishing to spread agriculture and people who fell trees for loggers. They are very shy Red pandas are small mammals with long, fluffy tails and red and white markings. Though they share a name with the more famous giant panda, they are not closely related. In fact, the name 'panda' was first applied to these animals, and not to the larger black-and-white bear.
