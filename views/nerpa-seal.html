Nerpa Seal

The Nerpa Seal or the Baikal seal has about 80,000 left on the world. They live only in the lake of Baikal. They are being killed for their furs. About 2,000 seals are killed for their fur a year and 1,500-4,000 a year die annually due to drowning poaching etc. The most serious threat in the future is global warming.
