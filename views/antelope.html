The Saiga Antelope is an antelope which can be defined with a few distinct features- a large humped nose, whihc helps it breathe during dust storms and other weather events, a thin, light brown colored coat, and long, thin legs. This antelope is facing major threats, which include habitat loss, poaching, and harsh winters. With a population of about 50,000, it is critically endangered and in dire need of protection.
