The Amsterdam Albatross is a related species to the albatross. The Amsterdam Albatross is found only on Amsterdam Island, French Southern Territories, in the southern Indian Ocean. The Amsterdam Albatross breeds once a year, and in the same spot every year. The adults have a medium to dark brown back and have a white underside and face mask.
